SecurePassGuard

SecurePassGuard is a password anomaly detection, alarm, and logging system. Suspiciously high motion detection count and incorrect access attempts? Sound the alarm by clicking on the button below. This will also email you a tracking record of all recent password attempts and loop the alarm sound.
